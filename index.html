What I do

I help technology companies with taking their idea from a napkin to a prototype or a production-ready product. I’m most passionate about creating playful physical one-off or small scale products that bring joy into lives of people who enjoy them.

To bring your idea to life, I use my wide range of experience in custom circuit design (Eagle) & PCB manufacturing, Raspberry Pi and Arduino, IoT and home automation as well as RF-based devices (WiFi, Bluetooth) or 3D modelling and printing (Fusion 360). Having spent most of my life building complex, scalable and secure web applications, I can also help with software architecture and writing both embedded and web software in C, Python or JavaScript.

When working with clients, I always strive to bring more to the table: not only building your idea, but also being a partner that helps you navigate the technical tradeoffs or helps you tackle a difficult challenge. Drawing from my experience working in agile teams in a fast-paced and deadline-driven environment, I can deliver your project on time individually or collaborate with your team.

I specialise in using technology to make ordinary items come to life: be it an interactive element of a set design for your next play, venue for your event, location of your cocktail club or just something to make your office bring more joy.

If your project requires skills that I haven’t acquired yet - reach out anyway, there is a good chance I know someone who has.
